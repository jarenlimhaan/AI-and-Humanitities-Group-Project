How to use Digi?
Using Digi is simple and user-friendly, designed to make your journey in digital banking as smooth as possible. Here’s a quick guide on how to get started:
  1. Start a Conversation: Open Digi on our website or app by clicking the chat icon.
  2. Choose Your Language: Select your preferred language for a seamless experience.
  3. Ask a Question: Ask Digi anything about online banking—from setting up an account to learning safe practices.
  4. Follow Step-by-Step Guidance: Digi provides clear instructions for online banking tasks, making it easy to follow along.
  5. Practice Scenarios: Build confidence by trying out real-life banking scenarios, like transferring money.
  6. Learn About Security: Get practical tips to understand online security and bank with confidence.
  7. Get Assistance Anytime: Digi is available 24/7 and can connect you to additional resources for further learning.
If you need more information, Digi can also guide you to relevant resources for further learning. Digi is your personal guide to mastering digital financial skills, helping you gain the confidence you need to navigate online banking with ease and security. Start your journey with Digi today!
